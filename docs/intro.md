# Welcome to Physical AI: A Hands-On Guide

Welcome to your journey into the fascinating world of **Physical AI**â€”where artificial intelligence meets the physical world through robotics, sensors, and intelligent machines.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to intelligent systems that don't just process dataâ€”they **perceive**, **reason**, and **act** in the real world. Unlike chatbots or recommendation engines that exist purely in software, Physical AI systems have bodies. They see through cameras, feel through sensors, and move through motors.

From robot vacuums navigating your home to autonomous vehicles driving city streets, Physical AI is already transforming how machines interact with our world.
